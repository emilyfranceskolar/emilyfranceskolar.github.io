Here to learn

Below are examples of how I used specific parts of design thinking; empathise, define and iteate in a pitch for a dating app.
My ideas was to match users via interests, tastes in people and spirit animals.

Empathise

I questioned why love is so important to us humans. This lead to reading about the subject of love, and relationships. From there I created a survey, as I only had a week to do this.

Define

From there I tried to dig deeper. I wanted to understand why online dating is often experienced as difficult. I stumbled upon Maslow's Hierachhy of Needs. I tried to build my idea from basic human needs.

Ideate

I had a look at different apps for online dating. I also tried to think out of the box. I attempted to make the app personal and fun. So spirit animals were born. In my testing the idea was well received, but I made some mistakes as well.
